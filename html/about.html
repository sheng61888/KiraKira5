About KiraKira

We empower Malaysian students to master SPM Mathematics through guided self-study, bite-sized explanations, and endless practice opportunities.

Our Story

Born From Real SPM Struggles

KiraKira started when a group of SPM graduates realized there was no single place to revise, drill past questions, and build exam confidence without expensive tuition. We teamed up with teachers, examiners, and university students to translate modern classroom strategies into an independent-learning platform that feels like a patient mentor.

Laser-Focused Content

Every lesson follows the latest KSSM syllabus for Modern & Additional Mathematics. We highlight what the examiner really wants and how to earn every method mark.

Independent, Not Isolated

Step-by-step walkthroughs, annotated solutions, and instant feedback help you study whenever you have time, while still feeling guided.

Practice That Builds Stamina

Auto-generated drills, topical quizzes, and full-length simulators make it easy to turn weak topics into strengths before the real paper.

How We're Different

KiraKira vs. Traditional Tuition

KiraKira Approach

  • Adaptive missions, streak tracking, and XP/badge rewards keep learners consistent daily.
  • Bilingual micro-lessons with side-by-side Bahasa Melayu + English terminology.
  • Instant quiz analytics, locked previews, and live platform metrics accessible 24/7.
  • Community threads, mission chips, and self-serve catalog so learners control pacing.

Typical Tuition Centre

  • Fixed lesson plan once a week; progress only checked during class.
  • Single-language delivery depending on tutor, limited translation support.
  • Worksheets and feedback depend on tutor availability; little transparency beforehand.
  • Contact limited to in-person sessions; no asynchronous community or mission guidance.
